Phase 01

Delivery

Gang Delivery
What is gang delivery?

Delivering a gang means putting it in the right hands — people who know what they're doing, who will commit to growth, and who will recruit. Handing it off to someone who isn't ready is a serious mistake.

How to find the right leader

  • → Open the F1 panel and go to the Unemployed group
  • → Look for players who previously held a gang rank (01, 02, 03, Management) and left
  • → Use city announcements to attract as many candidates as possible each day
👁

Ideal leader profile

Consistent presence, genuine desire to grow, good attitude, and the ability to engage people. They don't need to be perfect — but they need real drive that you can shape in the 00 slot.

✅

00 Validation

This is the decisive step — don't skip it. Watch whether they show up, call members together, don't vanish when needed, carry themselves well, and genuinely want to grow the gang.

📞

Minimum 5 in the call

Only hand over a compound to someone who brings at least 5 people to the call. With 5+, there's already a mini-hierarchy to recruit from and grow. Gangs starting with 2 never build consistency.

Common delivery mistakes
  • Handing off to any player without validation
  • Delivering a gang with fewer than 5 people in the call
  • Only focusing on the night shift — creates the illusion of a full city but builds no consistency
  • "Drop the knife and disappear" — without post-delivery follow-up, the gang collapses in 1–2 days

Post-delivery support (mandatory)

  • ✓ Guide the leader through the start of management
  • ✓ Help with the first recruiting runs
  • ✓ Show how to organize presence and reinforcement
  • ✓ Explain what needs to happen in the first few days
Phase 02

Engagement

Gang Engagement
Goal of engagement

Structure newly created gangs so they don't die right at the start. The goal isn't just growing the number — it's growing with organization, routine, and connection.

🤝

Team recruiting

Go to the peer and recruit alongside the gang. This creates momentum, shows organization, and encourages the leader to stay actively involved.

🌡

Internal culture

Numbers mean nothing if the environment is toxic. A new member who walks into a cold or hostile vibe will leave fast and never come back — destroying daily retention.

🤜

Engage as an ally

Come in as a friend, not as staff. When you show up as "staff checking in," the gang changes behavior artificially. As an ally, you see reality and can actually improve things.

Leadership analysis checklist

  • ? Is the leader present?
  • ? Do they follow the "be a good person" standard?
  • ? Do they show real interest in growing the gang?
  • ! Without leadership the gang may make noise for a bit, but won't sustain the community
Internal hierarchy

Sometimes the leader can't handle everything alone. Help build a 02, 01, 03 structure that's always present — a farm manager, a recruitment manager. A gang that depends solely on the 00 is fragile.

Early retention of new members

  • ✓ Check if the player came back the next day
  • ✓ Confirm they understood what the gang is about
  • ✓ See if they connected with someone
  • ✓ Verify they participated in something
  • ✓ Assess if they felt like they belonged
  • ✗ A player who joins today and vanishes tomorrow is not growth — it's empty rotation
Phase 03

Retention

Gang Retention
Goal of retention

Ensure gangs grow on a solid foundation and don't lose new members. The gang needs to stop being a temporary group and become a community with its own identity.

5-step process

  1. Wall Street integration
  2. Invisible entry into the gang
  3. Gang retention
  4. Hands-on teaching
  5. Transition to Activation
🏦

Wall Street integration

Wall Street is the fuel for accelerated growth. Orgs must align and work alongside Wall Street — they bring the warmed-up players. This division needs to be clear to everyone.

👻

Invisible entry

If the gang changes completely just because you showed up, the community isn't solid yet. The goal is to improve the environment without making the "crutch" visible.

📚

Hands-on teaching

Teaching isn't lecturing. It's showing in practice: how to receive a new member, pull action, maintain the vibe, stay organized, build a good daily routine.

Spreadsheet monitoring (mandatory)

  • 📊 How many people are online at any given moment
  • 📊 How many players each gang recruited per day

Track: delivery date · shift · 00 name · player count · growth speed · activation status

Monitoring isn't bureaucracy — it's how you see the truth. Running on assumptions is the worst thing you can do.

Phase 04

Activation

Gang Activation
What is activation

The gang isn't trying to exist — it exists. The goal now is to transform it into a strong, consistent structure with its own identity, hierarchy, and routine.

Rank 00

Leader

Holds the vision, standards, attitude, and overall direction of the gang.

Manager

Recruitment

Ensures a continuous flow of new members and consistent growth.

Manager

Farm

Sustains the operational and economic side of the gang.

Hierarchy rule

When these roles truly exist, the gang doesn't rest on one person's shoulders. This is what allows it to survive when the 00 is absent.

Identity & culture (mandatory)

  • 🎯 Its own identity
  • 🗣 Its own way of talking
  • 🤝 Its own way of welcoming people
  • ⚡ Its own way of acting
  • 🔄 Daily recruiting routine
  • 💪 Internal pride
  • 🏠 The feeling of "this gang is ours"
Continuous recruiting

A large gang that stops recruiting starts aging and weakening. There are always people leaving, cooling off, or going less active. The intake must keep going. When identity exists, a player who's proud of their gang doesn't leave — they invite others.
